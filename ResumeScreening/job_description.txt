Job Title: Data Analyst

Job Description:

We are looking for a detail-oriented Data Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for analyzing large datasets to identify trends, create reports, and support data-driven decision-making.

Responsibilities:

Collect, clean, and analyze data from various sources.
Create and maintain dashboards and reports.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand their data needs.
Present findings and insights to stakeholders.
Assist in the development of data models and forecasts.
Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field.
Proficiency in SQL and Excel; experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI) is a plus.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
Experience with programming languages such as Python or R is advantageous.
Preferred Qualifications:

Experience in a similar role within a tech or finance company.
Knowledge of machine learning concepts and techniques.
Familiarity with data warehousing concepts.
